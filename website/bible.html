Guided Light App

A daily inspiration & spiritual guidance mobile application built with Flutter. Features personalized daily reflections, Bible integration, offline functionality, and a calming celestial design for users seeking spiritual nourishment.

Sign Up for Testing Explore Features Legal & Support

Key Features

Discover what makes the Guided Light App a unique spiritual companion

Daily Personalized Guidance

Custom daily reflections and inspiration based on user's birth date. Personalized greetings that adapt to time of day and curated spiritual content that resonates with individual users.

Bible Integration

Daily Bible verse selection with proper references, full Bible navigation with search functionality, multiple Bible translations support, and verse bookmarking and sharing capabilities.

Calming User Experience

Celestial design with soft blues and gentle golds, smooth animations and transitions, light uplifting interface that promotes peace and reflection, and accessibility-focused design.

Offline Functionality

Complete offline access to daily guidance, local Bible content storage, data synchronization when online, and robust data management system for seamless experience.

Social & Sharing Features

Share inspirational verses and reflections, community features for spiritual growth, prayer request functionality, and content sharing across social platforms.

Cross-Platform

Built with Flutter for iOS and Android compatibility, offline-first approach with comprehensive offline support, performance optimized with fast loading times, and accessibility compliant design.

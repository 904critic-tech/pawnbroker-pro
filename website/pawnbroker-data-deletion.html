‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between data deletion and account deletion?

Data deletion removes specific data while keeping your account active. Account deletion removes your entire account and all associated data permanently.

Can I recover deleted data?

No. Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Make sure to export any important data before deletion.

How long does data deletion take?

Data is marked for deletion immediately, but complete removal takes up to 7 days to ensure thorough cleanup.

Will deleting data affect my app functionality?

Minimal impact. Your account will remain active and functional. You may need to reconfigure some settings after deletion.

Can I delete data selectively?

Yes. You can choose which specific data types to delete while keeping others intact.

What happens to my account after data deletion?

Your account remains active and functional. You can continue using the app normally, though some features may need to be reconfigured.

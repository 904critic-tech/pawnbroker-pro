üîß Common Issues & Solutions

App Won't Load or Crashes

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Restart the app completely
  3. Clear app cache: Settings > Apps > Guided Light > Storage > Clear Cache
  4. Restart your device
  5. Uninstall and reinstall the app

Bible Content Not Loading

  1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  2. Wait a few moments for content to load
  3. Try refreshing the screen by pulling down
  4. Check if your device has enough storage space
  5. Contact support if the issue persists

Ads Not Displaying Properly

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Ensure ads are not blocked by your device settings
  3. Check if you have any ad-blocking software enabled
  4. Restart the app
  5. Contact support if ads are inappropriate or not working

Search Function Not Working

  1. Make sure you have an internet connection
  2. Try different search terms
  3. Check spelling of book names or verses
  4. Wait for search results to load completely
  5. Contact support if search consistently fails

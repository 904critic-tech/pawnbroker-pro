Promotion Strategies

1

Optimize Your Profile

Create a compelling artist profile with high-quality images, detailed descriptions, and links to your social media.

  • • Use professional photos and artwork
  • • Write engaging artist bio
  • • Include social media links
  • • Add genre and mood tags
2

Use Descriptive Titles

Choose track titles that are descriptive and searchable to help content creators find your music.

  • • Include genre in title
  • • Add mood descriptors
  • • Use relevant keywords
  • • Keep titles memorable
3

Engage with Community

Actively participate in the platform community by commenting, liking, and sharing other artists' work.

  • • Comment on tracks you like
  • • Follow other artists
  • • Share music you enjoy
  • • Participate in discussions
4

Cross-Promote

Leverage your existing social media presence to drive traffic to your tracks on the platform.

  • • Share track links on social media
  • • Create behind-the-scenes content
  • • Collaborate with other artists
  • • Use platform-specific hashtags
